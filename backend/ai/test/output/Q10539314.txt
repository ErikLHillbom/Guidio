Jakobsgatan is quite a vibrant street in the heart of Norrmalm, Jon. It runs from Tegelbacken to Västra Trädgårdsgatan and is known for its lively entertainment scene. You’ll find places like Hamburger Börs and Centralpalatset here, which add to the cultural fabric of Stockholm. It’s a great spot to soak up the atmosphere and enjoy some local entertainment while you're exploring the city!
