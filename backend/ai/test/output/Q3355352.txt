If you’re exploring the cultural side of Stockholm, the Royal Institute of Art is definitely worth mentioning. Founded in 1735, this institution has a rich history and is dedicated to higher education in the arts. Originally part of the Royal Swedish Academy of Arts, it became independent in 1978. Today, it operates under the Ministry of Education and Research, continuing to influence art and education in Sweden. It’s a fascinating place to soak up the artistic heritage of the city, Jon!
