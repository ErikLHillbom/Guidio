You’ll want to check out the Vrak – Museum of Wrecks while you're in Stockholm, Jon. Opened in September 2021, this fascinating museum is located in Boat hangar No 2 at Galärvarvet on Djurgården. It’s all about maritime archaeology, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the Baltic Sea. 

One of the highlights is its focus on the nearly one hundred identified shipwrecks lying on the seabed, offering a unique glimpse into maritime history. It’s managed by the Maritime Museum, so you know it’s steeped in research and expertise. If you're into history and culture, this place is definitely worth a visit!
