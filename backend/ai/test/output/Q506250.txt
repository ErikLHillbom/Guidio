You know, Jon, Stockholm Municipality, or the City of Stockholm, is quite fascinating. It’s the most populous municipality in Sweden and even in the whole Nordic region, yet it has one of the smallest land areas. This unique combination makes it the second most densely populated area in the country!

As we explore the history, it's interesting to note that Stockholm has been a significant city since 1863, when the first local government acts came into effect. Over the years, it has expanded considerably, incorporating large areas in the early 20th century. Today, the city is divided into 14 districts, each with its own local governance, which helps manage various services and cultural activities.

Culturally, Stockholm is vibrant, blending a rich history with a modern urban lifestyle. The city is a hub for education and arts, with a high percentage of residents holding higher education degrees.
