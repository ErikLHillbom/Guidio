You’d be interested to know about the Palladium, Jon. This was a significant cinema in Stockholm, operating from 1918 to 1987. When it first opened, it was actually the largest cinema in Sweden, boasting over 1,200 seats! This place hosted many premieres for Svensk Filmindustri films, as well as international releases. Nowadays, the spot has transformed into Casino Cosmopol, but its rich history as a cultural hub still resonates in the area. It’s a fascinating piece of Stockholm's entertainment legacy!
