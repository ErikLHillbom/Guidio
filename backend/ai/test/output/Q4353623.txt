You might find 106.7 FM Rockklassiker particularly interesting, especially since you're in Stockholm, Jon! This rock radio station has been a significant part of the Swedish music scene since it started in 1994. Originally a local station, it has grown to become the second-largest rock station in Sweden by audience share.

What’s cool is that Rockklassiker was actually named "Radio Station of the Year" in 2001 by The Swedish Radio Academy. It primarily targets males aged 25 to 44 and has been a favorite among listeners, consistently performing well in the ratings over the years. If you’re a fan of rock music, tuning in could definitely give you a taste of Swedish culture!
