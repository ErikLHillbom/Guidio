Oh, you’ll love exploring Hamngatan, Jon! This vibrant street is a central artery in Stockholm, stretching from Sergels torg down to Nybroplan. As you stroll along, you’ll pass some iconic spots like the stylish NK department store and the beautiful Kungsträdgården park. It’s also lined with lovely areas like Norrmalmstorg and Berzelii Park, perfect for soaking in the city's culture. Plus, if you're curious about the city's transport history, the Djurgården line runs along this route, connecting some key points in the city. It's a great blend of history and everyday life!
