You know, Jon, if you're keen on history and culture, the Moderna Museet in Stockholm is a fantastic spot to check out. Opened in 1958, this state museum is situated on the picturesque island of Skeppsholmen. It features an impressive collection of both Swedish and international modern and contemporary art. You’ll find works by masters like Picasso and Dalí, alongside pieces from contemporary artists.

Interestingly, the museum also has a sculpture park with captivating installations from various artists, making it a lovely place to stroll around. Plus, it hosts temporary exhibitions, adding a dynamic aspect to your visit. If you love a blend of history with artistic flair, this could be a highlight of your trip!
