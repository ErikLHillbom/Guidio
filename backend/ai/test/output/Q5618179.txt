You’ll love the Gummeson Gallery, Jon! It’s a fascinating spot located right on Strandvägen in Stockholm. Established in 1912 by Carl Gummeson, it’s been a pivotal player in the contemporary art scene. The gallery quickly earned a reputation for championing Modern Art, showcasing works from legends like Wassily Kandinsky and Paul Klee early on.

Over the years, it’s featured iconic artists such as Edvard Munch and Andy Warhol too. Plus, it has been a platform for Swedish artists like Isaac Grünewald and Gösta Adrian-Nilsson. If you're into history and culture, this gallery is definitely worth a visit!
