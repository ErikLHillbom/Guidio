The Royal Swedish Academy of Engineering Sciences, or IVA, has quite the fascinating history, Jon! Founded in 1919 by King Gustaf V, it boasts the title of the world’s oldest academy of engineering sciences. This independent organization is all about fostering collaboration between business, research, and government, making it a pivotal player in Sweden's innovation landscape.

They focus on a variety of engineering disciplines, ranging from mechanical and electrical engineering to biotechnology and information technology, which shows just how broad their impact is. Additionally, the academy has a rich tradition of recognizing excellence through prestigious awards like the Large Gold Medal and the Gold Medal, encouraging advancements in these fields.

If you’re into history and culture, the Academy certainly reflects Sweden's commitment to engineering and innovation throughout the years!
