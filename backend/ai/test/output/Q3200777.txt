Have you ever heard of Kvasten, Jon? It's a fantastic ride known as "The Broom" at Gröna Lund amusement park in Stockholm. What makes it special is that it's an inverted roller coaster designed with families in mind. Built by Vekoma, it offers a thrilling experience without being too intense, making it perfect for those looking to enjoy some fun together. If you're into history and culture, Gröna Lund itself has a rich heritage, having opened its doors back in 1883. It’s a neat blend of excitement and tradition!
