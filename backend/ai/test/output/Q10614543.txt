You’ll find PK-huset right at the bustling intersection of Hamngatan and Norrlandsgatan in Norrmalm, Jon. This building is an interesting mix of office and retail space, and it’s home to the Swedish branch of Nordea, a prominent Nordic finance group. 

What's fascinating about PK-huset is its history — originally known as PK-bankens hus, it reflects the architectural style and urban development of Stockholm. The building stands as a testament to the city’s evolution over the years. If you're keen on exploring more about Stockholm's history and culture, this place is definitely worth a look!
