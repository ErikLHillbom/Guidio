You're going to love exploring Stureplan, Jon! This iconic public square in central Stockholm sits between Norrmalm and Östermalm and connects several major streets, making it a hub of activity. The area is known for its exclusive vibe, with some of the city's finest and most expensive restaurants and bars, like Sturehof and Spy Bar. 

Historically, Stureplan has become a symbol of luxury since its renovations in the 1980s, attracting a trendy crowd, including young business executives and celebrities. If you’re interested in culture, it’s also close to Humlegården park and the National Library of Sweden, adding a nice touch to your visit. Sounds like a great spot to soak in the history and culture of Stockholm!
