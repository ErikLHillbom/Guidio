You know, Jon, if you’re interested in history and culture, the General Industrial Exposition of 1866 in Stockholm is a fascinating topic. It was quite a significant event as the first international Art- and Industrial Exposition held in Sweden, showcasing a range of innovations and creativity. 

This exhibition ran from June 15 to October 14 and took place in the beautiful Industrial Hall at Kungsträdgården and the Nationalmuseum. Around 3,800 participants from Sweden and neighboring countries like Denmark, Norway, and Finland came together to showcase their advancements. It really marked an important moment in Swedish cultural history, reflecting the industrial progress of the time. If you're exploring more about historical exhibitions, this one definitely stands out!
