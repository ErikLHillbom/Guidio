You know, while we’re exploring Stockholm, the Petersen House is definitely worth a glance. Built between 1645 and 1659, this gem showcases the Dutch Baroque architectural style, which adds a unique flair to the city’s historical landscape. Designed by Christian Julius Döteber, it reflects the rich history and cultural influences that shaped Stockholm over the centuries. If you’re keen on history, Jon, this building embodies a fascinating era you might really enjoy!
