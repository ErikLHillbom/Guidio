If you're interested in history and culture, the Royal Swedish Academy of War Sciences is quite a fascinating spot, Jon. Founded in 1796 by Gustaf Wilhelm af Tibell, this academy serves as a vital forum for military and defense studies, focusing on national security issues. 

Membership is exclusive, capped at 160 members under the age of 62, which adds to its prestige. This institution has been influential in shaping Sweden's approach to military and defense matters over the centuries. Its historical significance and the caliber of its members, including notable figures like Anders Tegnell, make it a remarkable part of Sweden's cultural fabric. 

It's definitely worth a look if you're exploring the rich history of Stockholm!
