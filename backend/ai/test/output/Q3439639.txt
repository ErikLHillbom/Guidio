Ah, Kreditbanken, or Sveriges Kreditbank, is quite an interesting piece of history here in Stockholm. Founded way back in 1923 as Jordbrukarbanken, it played a significant role in the banking scene. In 1974, it merged with the Post Bank to become PK-Banken, which later acquired Nordbanken in 1980. Eventually, it adopted the Nordbanken name and became part of Nordea, which is a major player today.

Interestingly, this location is also tied to the infamous Norrmalmstorg robbery, which is where the term "Stockholm syndrome" originated.
