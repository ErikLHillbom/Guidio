You’ll love checking out Teatergrillen, Jon. This charming Swedish restaurant is located at Nybrogatan 3 in Stockholm and has quite a bit of history behind it, having been established by the renowned Tore Wretman. It’s a great spot to experience traditional Swedish cuisine while soaking in the cultural vibe of the city. If you appreciate the stories behind the places you visit, Teatergrillen certainly has its own unique narrative!
