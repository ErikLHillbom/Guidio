Have you heard about the S/S Orion, Jon? This fascinating steam-powered ship is moored on the western shore of Skeppsholmen in central Stockholm. It’s actually the oldest vessel of its kind still in Sweden and serves as a museum ship now.

Built in 1929, it initially worked for the Swedish Maritime Administration, inspecting lighthouses and laying out navigational buoys. The Orion was actively used until 1979, and since 1993, it’s been showcasing its rich history to visitors. If you enjoy history and culture, this ship definitely offers a unique glimpse into Sweden's maritime past!
