You'll find the Museum of Far Eastern Antiquities quite captivating, especially given your interest in history and culture. Established in 1926, the museum is nestled in the historical Tyghuset on Skeppsholmen. Johan Gunnar Andersson, a notable Swedish archaeologist, was the founding director, and he laid the groundwork for what has become a rich collection.

The museum showcases a diverse array of artifacts from East Asia, including Japan, Korea, India, and China. You can explore everything from ancient archaeological pieces to classical art and even contemporary cultural displays. Some highlights include stunning sculptures like a Jain Tirthankara from the 8th century and an exquisite Ganesha from the Chola dynasty.
