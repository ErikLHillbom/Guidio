Have you heard about Marie Cederschiöld University College? It’s this fascinating private institution in Stockholm, and it really has a rich history tied to human health and welfare. Founded in 1851, it started with Sweden's first nurse training course, which is pretty cool, right? 

The college focuses on education in nursing, social work, and theology, along with research in caring science and civil society studies. So, Jon, if you're interested in how education intersects with social issues, this place has a unique profile shaped by its diaconal roots. The campus, located in Södermalm, offers a vibrant environment that reflects the cultural dynamics of the area. It’s definitely worth checking out if you want to dive deep into the historical and cultural aspects of education in Sweden!
